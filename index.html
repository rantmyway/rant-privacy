Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

Our Commitment to Privacy

At Rant, we take your privacy seriously. Our app is built on the principle of anonymous expression, and we're committed to protecting your privacy.

Information We Collect

To provide our service, we collect:

  • Account information (username and password for authentication only)
  • Content you choose to share (Rants)
  • Interaction data (Echoes)

How We Protect Your Privacy

  • All Rants are posted with randomly generated IDs
  • We never collect or display personal information
  • Your account information is encrypted and securely stored
  • We don't track your location or personal identifiers

Data Usage

We use your data solely to:

  • Provide the Rant service
  • Maintain app functionality
  • Ensure community safety
  • Improve user experience

Data Sharing

We never sell your data. We only share information:

  • When required by law
  • To protect user safety
  • To prevent abuse of the platform

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your account data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Request data export

Contact Us

For privacy concerns or questions, contact us at: support@rantmyway.com

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as needed. We will notify users of any material changes via the app.
